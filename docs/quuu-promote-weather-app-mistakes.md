## Why this topic fits OZSNAP

The article starts from a broad problem shared by almost anyone who checks the weather: treating a daily maximum and one icon as a complete plan. It then turns feels-like temperature, hourly rain, gusts, UV and changing dayparts into practical decisions about clothing, timing, shelter and what to pack.

OZSNAP appears as the useful next step rather than the subject of a sales pitch. Contextual links lead readers to the live city decision view, the 10-level outfit guide, Australian city context and optional photography planning.
