What the lists include and exclude

Each guide records Google Australia’s regular web results with English selected and personalisation turned off. The exact query, search date and location reported by Google appear on the page.

Included

Independent websites

A photographer or studio website offering family sessions in the named city or service area.

Excluded

Other result types

Ads, map listings, social profiles, Reddit discussions and third-party directories do not enter the ten-site order.

Order

Observed, not rescored

Eligible sites keep the relative order in which they appeared. OZSNAP does not add stars or rearrange them.

Limits

A dated snapshot

Google results vary by location and can change. These guides are evidence of one documented search, not a permanent award.
