# OZSNAP site structure

## Canonical URL

- `https://ozsnap.com/` is the only indexable page.

## Page sections

- `#today` - live city conditions and outfit recommendation
- `#forecast` - seven-day outfit forecast
- `#best-light` - golden hour, sunset and cloud-aware photo advice
- `#shoot-planner` - date and session-length planning tool

Hash sections are not separate URLs and are not listed in the sitemap.

## Search files

- `sitemap.xml` lists the canonical homepage only.
- `robots.txt` allows crawling and declares the sitemap.
- `404.html` is excluded from search with `noindex`.
