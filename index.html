    Read the sky

    Cloud is part of the light

    The useful question is not whether cloud is good or bad. It is what the sky lets you photograph.

    01

    Clear sky

    Wait for shape and colour

    Backlight your subject late, or use open shade while the sun is still high.

    02

    Broken cloud

    Watch the western gap

    High cloud can hold colour when a clear strip near the horizon lets warm light through.

    03

    Overcast

    Use soft portrait light

    Faces stay even with fewer hard shadows. Find colour and a clean background for separation.

    04

    After rain

    Look for reflection

    Wet pavement carries signs and sky into the frame. Work from a sheltered edge and keep the lens dry.

    How recommendations work

    Forecast data, translated honestly

    OZSNAP starts with apparent temperature rather than the headline temperature, then checks rain, wind, UV and the day’s temperature range. The ten outfit levels are practical guidance, not a safety rule: activity, exposure time and personal comfort still change what feels right.

    Photography advice combines calculated sun angles with forecast cloud, rain and wind. It describes potential rather than promising a sunset. Conditions are refreshed after 30 minutes, and saved forecast data is shown when the weather service is temporarily unavailable.

    Good to know

    Before you head out

    Is golden hour always exactly one hour?

    No. Its length changes with latitude and season. OZSNAP calculates the evening window from the sun’s angle for the selected city and date.

    Why can cloudy weather be better for portraits?

    Cloud scatters direct sunlight, reducing hard shadows under the eyes and nose. Heavy cloud can soften colour, so stronger clothing or background colour often helps.

    Does “Use my location” give exact local weather?

    It selects the nearest OZSNAP city forecast. It does not replace official local observations, warnings or emergency advice.

    How fresh is the weather?

    Forecast conditions are checked when the page opens and refreshed after 30 minutes. A recent saved forecast is used if the weather service is temporarily unavailable.
